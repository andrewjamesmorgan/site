Car Parking

We provide off street secure parking. If you would like to use this facility please call us to pre book the parking space in advance of your visit. You would need to go to the drive-in iron gates situated on Vansittart Road (next to St Stephens Church). Please then use the key pad instructions on the right hand side of the gates to gain access and be directed to your reserved parking bay. Alternatively there is on street parking for clients but this is easily accessible and out of town. It is 20p per hour. This can be on Vansittart Rd or Arthur Rd or any of the side roads running off these. There is also a public carpark off Arthur Rd by the coach station and some 5 minutes walk from the office. We are a 5 minute drive from the M4 junction 5 and 1 minute from the Windsor relief road.
