Civil Litigation

We are able to assist in all aspects of civil disputes including negotiations and dispute resolution.

Areas most commonly covered are:-

  1. Consumer rights – where you have purchased items and you have a dispute with the seller
  2. Property disputes including neighbour disputes, building disputes, expert reports disputes
  3. Commercial disagreements – contracts between companies
  4. Debt collection
  5. Landlord and Tenant disputes

No matter what type of dispute you have, litigation is highly stressful.  We aim to listen to your concerns, provide you with the advice and procedural steps to follow.  We will act on your instructions and keep you informed of progress at every stage. 

Inevitably the cost of litigation will be of a concern.  We will provide you with estimates on a regular basis, allowing you to budget for the costs.  Wherever possible, negotiations and proposals for settlement will take account of the costs and the risk of increasing those costs disproportionately to the potential gain if matters do not settle.

The aim is to empower you with the knowledge of the law, the risks and the costs and allow you to make an informed decision.
