Allwyn Sanger have a total of over 30 years experience in practicing law including with a top City family law firm.

We provide quality legal services with a focus on family law including prenuptials, cohabitation, divorce, financial matters (including pensions, property and investment portfolios) and issues relating to children (including parental responsibility). In addition, the firm practices in wills & probate (including tax advice, employment law and general civil litigation.

The firm is based at Eton Bridge in the heart of Windsor, servicing clients in Eton, Windsor, Datchet and elsewhere in the Thames Valley.

We are commited to providing excellence in our practice by understanding our client's needs and providing a bespoke service through the use of modern methods and technology.

We seek to differentiate ourselves from other solicitors by empowering our clients to control the level of service - specifying what they want the solicitor to perform versus what they are comfotable tackling themselves. This allows the client to get maximum value from their solicitor's time.

We are conveniently located next to the bridge connecting Windsor and Eton. We are also just a short drive from the M4 with easy access to London and the Thames Valley.
