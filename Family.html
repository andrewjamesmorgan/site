Family Law - Family, Divorce and Children

We are able to help on all aspects of matrimonial matters including divorce, co-habitation, Civil Partnerships, pre nuptials and children.

All our clients' matters are dealt with using a sensitive approach for their best interests.

Allwyn Sanger are highly experienced in dealing with complex matters, largely relating to high net worth assets and often in cases which have a cross jurisdictional aspect.

Litigation should be the last resort. It is expensive, stressful and causes delay in seeking closure at a particularly difficult time.
 
We are members of Resolution – "First for Family law", and we adhere to the Resolution practice code by approaching cases in a non confrontational manner and encourage constructive early settlements wherever possible by negotiation and mediation.

Ash Sanger is also an accredited member of the Law Society's Family Law Panel and is a Director and a Solicitor of England and Wales.

Our holistic approach allows us to listen to your needs, concerns and what you would want to achieve by way of a settlement. We will conduct your case to minimise further potential disputes.
